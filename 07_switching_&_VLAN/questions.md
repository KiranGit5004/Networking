### What is switching?

✅ Answer:
Switching is the process of forwarding data within a LAN using MAC addresses.

### What is MAC address table?

✅ Answer:
A table in switch that maps MAC addresses to ports.

### What happens if MAC is unknown?

✅ Answer:
Switch floods the frame to all ports.

### What is VLAN?

✅ Answer:
VLAN is a logical segmentation of a network into smaller networks.

### Why VLAN is used?

✅ Answer:
For security, reducing broadcast, and better network management.

### What is access vs trunk port?

✅ Answer:
Access port connects end devices, trunk carries multiple VLANs.

### Can devices in different VLAN communicate?

✅ Answer:
No, unless routing is used.

### What is broadcast storm?

✅ Answer:
Excessive broadcast traffic causing network failure.

### how VLAN reduce the broadcast traffic?
 A VLAN reduces broadcast traffic by dividing the network into small broadcast domains.
 Devices within the same VLAN can broadcast to each other but that traffic doesnt reach other VLANs, minimizing unnecessary traffic across the network.
